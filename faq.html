YOUR FIRST SESSION

We welcome riders from 4 years old. There's no upper age limit — sessions cater for all ages and abilities.

No booking required — just turn up. We do recommend getting in touch beforehand so we know to expect you and can make sure someone is on hand to welcome you.

All new riders attend Level 1 first, regardless of age. This runs on Thursdays, 17:15–18:15. Coaches will assess your child and work out the best path forward from there.

If your child is an experienced rider and can provide proof of their level, contact us and we can discuss the most suitable session.

Our schedule can change at short notice and sessions may be cancelled due to weather or other circumstances. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram for the latest updates — cancellations are posted by 4pm on the day. Always check before you travel.

COACHING & SESSIONS

We run coached sessions across the week, covering all levels from complete beginner through to national standard. See the full session schedule on the main site for days, times and what each session involves.

All our coaches are British Cycling BMX-qualified:

  • Quillan Isidore — Levels 3 & 4. 2012 UCI World Champion (U16) and multiple-time British National Champion.
  • Coach Michael — Level 2. Specialises in building confidence in new and young riders.
  • Coach Karl — Level 1. Focused on introducing beginners to the sport.
KIT & EQUIPMENT
  • Full-face helmet — mandatory at all times while riding
  • Gloves — mandatory
  • Long sleeves and long trousers — no shorts
  • Knee pads, elbow pads and body armour — recommended

All essential safety gear can be hired from us for £3 per session. See our Bikes & Kit Guide for advice on what to buy.

Yes — bike and kit hire is available for £3 per session. This includes a bike, full-face helmet, pads and gloves. We have a full range of sizes from micro minis to cruisers. Hire is on a first-come, first-served basis so arriving early is recommended.

MEMBERSHIP

Membership is handled through British Cycling. There is a non-refundable £1.00 application fee. By joining you agree to our Code of Conduct.

  • Junior membership — £10 per year (age 4–16)
  • Adult membership — £15 per year (age 16+)
  • Family membership — £30 per year (2 adults + 1 junior, or 1 adult + up to 3 juniors)
  • Discounted session rate (£3 vs £5 for non-members)
  • Insurance cover through British Cycling
  • Access to all coaching levels
  • Eligibility to compete in club, regional and national races
GET INVOLVED

We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers — no prior experience needed. Get in touch at merseysidebmx@gmail.com and we'll be happy to chat about how you can help.

Sponsoring Merseyside BMX Club is a great way to support local youth development and gain brand exposure within the BMX community. Visit our sponsors page to find out more, or get in touch directly.
