GETTING STARTED

We welcome riders from as young as 4 years old. The key requirement is that they can ride a bike standing up — but this isn't always essential. We also welcome younger children on balance bikes to get a feel for the BMX environment. There's no upper age limit — sessions cater for all ages and abilities.

You can just turn up — no booking required for your first visit. We do recommend getting in touch beforehand so we know you're coming and can make sure someone is on hand to welcome you.

Your first session is free. After that, sessions cost £3 for members and £5 for non-members. Bike and kit hire is an additional £3 per session.

The best place to start is our Thursday Level 1 coaching session, 17:15–18:15. It's designed specifically for new riders — coaches will assess your child and provide tailored guidance from day one. Please get in touch before attending so we can expect you.

You're also welcome to come and watch a Tuesday or Thursday session before joining, to get a feel for the club.

Yes — we require a parent or guardian to remain on-site during all sessions. You're welcome to watch and cheer from the sidelines.

BMX racing develops physical fitness, coordination and discipline in a fun, outdoor environment. It's also a brilliant way to make friends and build confidence. Our club offers a structured pathway from complete beginner right through to national-level competition — there's always a next step to aim for.

MEMBERSHIP

Membership is handled through British Cycling. We offer Junior (age 4–16) and Adult (age 16+) memberships. There is a non-refundable £1.00 application fee. By joining you agree to our Code of Conduct.

  • Junior membership — £10 per year
  • Adult membership — £15 per year
  • Discounted session rate (£3 vs £5 for non-members)
  • Insurance cover through British Cycling
  • Eligibility to compete in club, regional and national races
  • Access to all coaching levels
  • Ability to order personalised club kit

RACING

Start by attending our regular sessions and building confidence on the track. Once you're comfortable, our club race series is the perfect first step — it's low pressure, open to all abilities and a great introduction to competitive BMX before moving up to regional events.

  • Novice: Perfect for beginners. Guidance is on hand from officials and volunteers. You can compete at novice level for one full season before moving up to expert. A British Cycling bronze membership is sufficient.
  • Expert: Requires a British Cycling race licence (silver membership or above) and a MYLAPS transponder. More competitive but still great fun.

BMX age categories run from 1st January to 31st December. Simply subtract your child's birth year from the current year. For example, a rider born in 2013 racing in 2025 would compete in the 12-year-old category (2025 − 2013 = 12).

Race licences are available through British Cycling. A bronze membership and provisional licence are sufficient for novice racing. For expert-level regional races, a silver membership is required.

  • Club races — our own series, open to all, ideal for first-time racers
  • Regional races — British Cycling North Region series, April to October
  • National Series — riders who complete 5 regional rounds at expert level qualify
  • British Championships — "The Brits", held on the August bank holiday. The UK's top event

Races go ahead in all weather unless the track is deemed unsafe. Always check our Facebook and Instagram on the morning of a race day for any updates.
