PRIVACY POLICY

This policy explains what personal data Merseyside BMX Club collects, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law.

Last updated: April 2026. This policy applies to the Merseyside BMX Club website at merseysidebmx.co.uk and any associated online services.

WHO WE ARE

Merseyside BMX Club is a volunteer-run community sports club based at Knowsley Leisure and Culture Park, Longview Drive, Huyton, Liverpool, L36 6EG.

We are the data controller for any personal data collected through this website. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at merseysidebmx@gmail.com.

WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

Contact form

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • The subject of your enquiry
  • The content of your message

This data is processed by Web3Forms (web3forms.com) and delivered to our club email inbox. We do not store this data beyond normal email retention.

Shop orders

When you place an order through our club shop, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Payment details — these are processed directly by SumUp and never seen or stored by us
  • Order details including product, size, rider name, and any personalisation information you provide

Payment processing is handled by SumUp (sumup.com). We receive a confirmation of your order and payment but do not have access to your full card details at any point. Confirmed order details are stored in a secure Google Sheet accessible only to club administrators.

Waiver form

Our Code of Conduct waiver is collected via Google Forms. The data you submit there is covered by Google's privacy policy and is accessible to club administrators only.

Website usage

We do not use analytics software or tracking cookies on this website. No cookies are set by us, and no browsing behaviour is recorded.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use the data we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries submitted via the contact form
  • To process and fulfil shop orders, including passing personalisation details to our kit supplier
  • To maintain records of waivers and membership as required for club administration and safeguarding

We do not use your data for marketing, and we do not send unsolicited emails. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any third party except as described below.

THIRD PARTIES WE SHARE DATA WITH

We share data with the following third-party services only where necessary to deliver our services:

  • Web3Forms — processes contact form submissions and forwards them to our email. Privacy policy: web3forms.com/privacy
  • SumUp — processes payments for shop orders securely. Privacy policy: sumup.com/privacy
  • Google Sheets — stores confirmed order records, accessible only to club administrators. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Forms — collects waiver data. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Our kit supplier — we share rider name, size and personalisation details with our supplier solely for the purpose of producing ordered items. No contact or payment data is shared.

All third-party providers are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection law.

HOW LONG WE KEEP YOUR DATA

  • Contact form enquiries — retained in our email inbox and deleted when no longer needed, typically within 12 months
  • Shop orders — order records are kept for up to 3 years for accounting and dispute resolution purposes
  • Waivers — retained for the duration of a rider's membership and for a reasonable period thereafter

YOUR RIGHTS

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data where there is no legitimate reason to retain it
  • Right to object — you can object to our processing of your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at merseysidebmx@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

CHILDREN'S DATA

Many of our members are under 18. Where we collect data relating to a child, we rely on parental consent and club membership consent obtained through our waiver process. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13 through this website without parental involvement.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately at merseysidebmx@gmail.com.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
