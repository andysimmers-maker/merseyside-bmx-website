Process

HOW IT WORKS

1
ADD TO BASKET
Select your products, choose sizes and enter your rider's personalisation details: name, race number and age.
2
PAY SECURELY
Checkout via Stripe. Payment is taken immediately and you'll receive an email confirmation of your order.
3
BULK ORDER
Your order is added to the club's monthly bulk order with our supplier. All personalisation is handled by us.
4
COLLECTION
We'll let you know when your items arrive and arrange collection at the track. Allow a few weeks after the order date.
Please note: Orders are placed monthly. Once your payment is made, your spot in the next order is confirmed. Allow 3–5 weeks from the order date for delivery to the club.
