Who We Are

ABOUT THE CLUB

A welcoming, inclusive BMX club based at Knowsley Leisure and Culture Park in Huyton, Liverpool. Run entirely by volunteers who are passionate about the sport and their community.

Merseyside BMX Club has been a cornerstone of BMX racing in the North Region since 2012. We provide weekly coaching sessions, race events, and a structured pathway for riders of every age and ability — from complete beginners to national-level competitors.

Whether you're looking to try BMX for the first time or develop your race craft, we have sessions to suit you. Our team of experienced coaches and volunteers is committed to the highest standards of safety and inclusivity.

We look forward to seeing you at the track.

Become a Member

JOIN THE CLUB

Whether you're brand new to BMX or looking to race at national level, becoming a club member is the first step to getting the most out of Merseyside BMX.

Membership is handled through British Cycling. Please note there is a non-refundable £1.00 application fee. By joining you also agree to our Code of Conduct.

Sessions are supervised by first aid qualified volunteers and track marshals. Bike hire, full-face helmets and all necessary safety equipment are available so you can get going straight away.

Expert Guidance

OUR COACHES

All sessions are led by British Cycling BMX-qualified coaches. Our main coaching night is Thursday with other sessions available across the week and weekend.

Level 3 & 4 Coach
QUILLAN ISIDORE
One of Britain's most decorated BMX racers — 2012 UCI World Champion (U16), multiple-time British National Champion and Team GB athlete. Now retired from elite competition, Quillan brings that wealth of experience to our club, providing coaching for our regional and national level riders. His focus is on building self-belief, consistency and the technical tools needed to compete at the very highest level.
Level 2 Coach
